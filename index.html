Diets

MyNetDiary supports a variety of diet plans to suit your lifestyle and goals:

  • Calorie Counting: The classic approach to weight management.
  • Low-Carb: Reduce carbohydrate intake for faster results.
  • Keto: High-fat, low-carb diet to achieve ketosis.
  • Vegan: Plant-based diet excluding all animal products.
  • Vegetarian: Diet excluding meat but may include dairy and eggs.
  • Custom Diets: Set your own macro ratios and goals.

Each diet plan comes with dietitian-approved recipes and educational articles.
